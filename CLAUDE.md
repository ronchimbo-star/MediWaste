# MediWaste Project Guidelines

## SEO Page Generation Standards

All SEO pages generated via the AI content tool MUST follow these guidelines. The edge function `generate-seo-content` enforces this via prompt, and the page template (`src/pages/SeoPage.tsx`) renders the standard layout.

### Layout Structure (in order)

1. **H1** — Must include target keyword + location (e.g. "Clinical Waste Collection in Worthing")
2. **Published/Updated date** — Auto-generated by the page template
3. **Intro paragraph** — Max 150 words summarising the service for that location
4. **Trust signal module** — After intro, before main content:
   - "Rated Excellent by [location] practices" (4.8+ stars)
   - 2 short customer testimonials specific to the area
   - Accreditations: Environment Agency, Safe Contractor, ISO 14001
5. **Key services table** — 3 columns: Service, Description, Frequency
6. **Our collection process** — 3 simple steps
7. **Local relevance section** — Local landmarks, authorities, businesses, postcodes
8. **Compliance & trust section** — Compliance guarantee box, client types, EA audit statement
9. **FAQ section** — Minimum 4 questions (mandatory questions listed below)
10. **CTA block** — Added automatically by page template (do NOT include in AI content)

### Mandatory FAQ Questions (localised)

- "How much does clinical waste collection cost in [location]?"
- "What types of clinical waste do you collect in [location]?"
- "Are you registered with the Environment Agency?"
- "How quickly can you start collections in [location]?"
- Plus 1-2 additional relevant questions

### Styling & Tone

- UK English spelling (colour, centre, licence, organised)
- No jargon — write for beauticians, aesthetic practitioners, GP managers, salon owners
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Bullet points for lists (not dense text)
- 1,500-1,700 minimum words (not including testimonials/FAQ)
- Professional but accessible, direct and factual
- Do NOT use "transform your waste management" or marketing fluff
- Do NOT repeat "clinical waste management is a critical service" across pages

### Local Relevance (mandatory)

- 2-3 sentences mentioning specific local landmarks, streets, or areas
- Local authority and relevant waste policies
- Typical local businesses served
- Nearby villages or postcode areas if location is a town

### Trust & Authority Signals

- Compliance guarantee box: "All waste transfer notes and consignment notes provided within 48 hours of collection."
- "Our clients in [location] include:" (GP surgeries, dental clinics, beauty salons, veterinary practices)
- "MediWaste is audited annually by the Environment Agency and holds a registered waste carrier licence (upper tier)."

### CTA (localised, handled by page template)

- Title: "Get your free, no-obligation quote for [location] today"
- Phone: 0800 046 9806 (national freephone)
- "We can usually start collections within 7 days in the [location] area"

### Things to Avoid

- Broken or placeholder links
- Generic environmental theory — focus on collection, compliance, and service
- Marketing fluff — be direct and factual
- Duplicate intros across pages
- Internal content CTAs (the template adds the standard CTA)

### Schema Markup

The page template automatically generates:
- **Article** schema (headline, dates, publisher)
- **LocalBusiness** schema (MediWaste, phone, area served)
- **FAQPage** schema (extracted from FAQ section in content)

### Meta Tags

Each page renders unique crawler-visible meta tags:
- `<title>` — from meta_title
- `<meta name="description">` — from meta_description
- `<meta name="keywords">` — from AI-generated keywords
- `<meta name="robots" content="index,follow">`
- `<link rel="canonical">`
- Open Graph tags (og:title, og:description, og:image)
- Twitter Card tags

### CSS Classes for Content Boxes

The `article-content` class supports these styled boxes:
- `.info-box` — blue background, for informational callouts
- `.success-box` — green background, for compliance guarantees
- `.warning-box` — red background, for important warnings
- `.note-box` — yellow background, for notes
- `.highlight-box` — purple background, for highlights
- `.cta-box` — red gradient, for in-content CTAs (avoid using — template CTA is preferred)
